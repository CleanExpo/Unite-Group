## Section 1 — Phone diagnosis (free)

### Free phone diagnosis

Talk to the CCW team first. In many cases, technicians can diagnose 99% of problems over the phone [TBC-Toby]. That means less guesswork, faster triage, and a better path to repair.

What to have ready:

- machine model
- symptoms
- when the issue started
- any error lights, leaks, noises, or performance changes
- what has already been checked
