# OFFER: NRPG

**For:** Australian restoration contractors who want matched work without phone-based selling

NRPG is a marketing-agency model for contractors: the platform connects clients with contractors, and communication stays online.

## What they get
1. Matched leads through an online-forms-only workflow.
2. Contractor exposure in a system that does not rely on phone calls.
3. A pricing tier that matches the population band of the service area.

## Price anchor
Use the existing code pricing model:
- Rural (<50k): **$395/mo**
- Semi-rural (50k–200k): **$595/mo**
- Tier 1 (200k–400k): **$795/mo**
- Tier 2 (400k–1M): **$995/mo**
- Tier 3 (>1M): **$1,095/mo**

## What is included
- Online forms as the contact path
- Email support via support@disasterrecovery.com.au
- AI bot messenger support model
- Dynamic SEO pages generated on demand with contractor sign-ups
- Contractor recruitment workflow for Stage 6 beta testing

## What is missing
- No phone-number sales model
- No promise of exclusive lead volume
- No claim that every area or contractor is already filled

## 12-month proof claim
The Stage 6 contractor recruitment docs show the model is already being actively tested with contractors, with a target of 10 active contractors, 20 invitations, and online-only coordination. The core pricing tiers are already defined in code, and the contact model is explicitly forms-led.

## Why this matters
The buyer is a contractor who wants to be matched with work, not to run a sales office. The offer should stay aligned with the existing no-phone, online-forms-only model.
