## 4. Approach — plain language first

A borrower (Gavin: 60, Cardinal Health, ~$150–175k total income, Miami unit bought $290k now worth $800–950k, super $360–380k in a Macquarie Wrap pension, $50–60k carry-forward concessional, saves $2–4k/month) refinances into **one master facility split into separate lines**. [VERIFIED — Gavin L23.] The home portion is a **GISI home line** — "Goes In, Stays In": no redraw, so money paid in cannot drift back out — while **retaining an offset account against the home line** (Gavin keeps his "fun fitness and food" offset and wants it added to the GISI). [VERIFIED — GISI + offset retained: "He'd like to add the offset account to the GiSI account. I have no problems with that"; home line "would have an offset account, which could have a balance of anywhere between seven and twenty thousand" Gavin L70, L73. NOTE: this **corrects the draft's fabricated `offset=false` invariant** — GISI removes *redraw*, not *offset*.] Alongside it sit non-deductible purpose lines (a $30k holiday LOC, a $70k car LOC) and **exactly one $100k deductible investment LOC** for income-producing assets. [VERIFIED — $30k holiday + $70k car limits Gavin L70-71; $100k investment LOC limit Gavin L73.] The non-deductible total is **$400k** ($300k home limit with $270k debt + $30k holiday + $70k car); the **$100k investment LOC is additional**, so the master facility is ~$500k. [VERIFIED — "overall he would have four hundred thousand dollars in non tax deductible limits, that would be made up of a thirty thousand dollar holiday limit, a seventy thousand dollar car limit. And a three hundred thousand dollars home loan limit" Gavin L73; investment LOC separate at L73. This **corrects the draft's "$400k / 4-line" master-limit conflation**.]

Each quarter the borrower does a budget, finds surplus (Gavin: $2–4k/month), and pays it into the GISI home line. As headroom builds, the rules direct money toward super as a concessional contribution and **draw a matching amount from the investment LOC into growth assets** — converting non-deductible "bad" debt into deductible "good" debt over time. [VERIFIED — "$4,000/month in savings pays down non-deductible home debt while drawing the same amount from the investment LOC to invest, converting bad debt into good debt" Gavin L62; "The line of credit would match ideally the savings that Gavin is pushing into his home loan" L73.]

**Fitzy** greets the borrower, presents the rules, tracks compliance per rule, runs the quarterly cadence, ingests the budget (Frollo open banking + MoneySmart) and payslips (emailed each ~3 months), and computes a **refund tier**: the $1,000 annual fee is refunded in $250 steps, one step per 5 rules followed. [VERIFIED — greet/rules L117; budget engine L117/L120; payslip-every-3-months cadence + $1,000/yr fee L126. NOTE: the $250-per-5-rules refund formula is from the **task brief**, not located verbatim in the re-read files → see risk #17.] **Fitzy presents, tracks, nudges, and computes the refund — it makes no credit or advice decision; humans (broker, planner) act.** [INFERENCE — basis: ACL accountability with the broker (SOW L142-143) and AFSL advice boundary (REG Anchor 4) forbid an unlicensed agent making those calls; Fitzy's corpus role is limited to greeting + presenting rules (L117).]

Three legal facts shape the build as hard walls, not footnotes:
1. **Credit assistance is regulated.** Arranging this split for a consumer needs an ACL/authorised rep + a documented responsible-lending workflow; the app must not arrange credit itself. [VERIFIED — SOW L142-143; REG Anchor 1.]
2. **A personalised super/ETF plan is personal financial advice.** A rules engine reading the borrower's own super balance to recommend contributions/ETF actions is personal advice → AFSL. The app stays on the **factual-information / general-advice** side, or sits behind a licensed adviser's SOA. [VERIFIED — REG Anchor 4.]
3. **Part IVA red zone.** The "sweep all repayments to the home sub-account while capitalising investment-line interest" pattern is what TR 98/22 + *Hart* disallow. The product must be **designed so investment-line interest is serviced in cash, facilities stay genuinely separate, and every investment-LOC draw is use-traced to an income-producing asset**. [VERIFIED — REG Anchor 2.]

[VERIFIED — broker ID document list Gavin L56; ACL accountability SOW L142-143; ID/AML excluded from AI layer ITR plaud L59-60. NOTE: "Mortgage House" is named only in the task brief, not the re-read corpus (corpus names the lender broker as Home Loan Essentials / "Jack at Home Loan Essentials") → see risk #4.]

**Phase 4 — Advice-boundary hardening (gated on AFSL decision).**
Either (a) integrate a licensed adviser's SOA/FSG flow for personalised super/ETF guidance, or (b) lock the app to factual-information/general-advice only with prominent warnings and no personalised recommendation logic. **DoD:** the chosen path is signed by a licensed adviser; no personalised super/ETF recommendation ships without AFSL coverage; general-advice warnings + FSG (where required) render. [VERIFIED — REG Anchor 4; SOW risk register flags AFSL/AFCA L38.]

## 7. Security & cost guardrails

- **No-TFN/No-ID boundary (structural).** No route, table, log, or AI prompt in this project may accept or persist a TFN or government ID. [VERIFIED — boundary + AML/Privacy rationale ITR plaud L53, L59-60, L86. - **Trust-zone split.** The Fitzy/budget/behaviour/compliance layer (no PII) and the broker KYC/AML surface are separate trust zones; the only link is an opaque token. ID/AML PII never crosses into Fitzy. [VERIFIED — ITR plaud L59-60, L86; broker ID collection Gavin L56.]
- **Secrets.** Supabase service-role key, Frollo API key, RP Data credentials, and any LLM key live in this project's env (Vercel/Supabase secrets), never in repo, never client-side. Sydney data residency for PII-at-rest, encrypted at rest, RLS tenant scoping. [VERIFIED — data-residency/PII in SOW risk register L38; Frollo/RP Data named L120/L75. [INFERENCE — "encrypted at rest / RLS scoping" are standard Supabase posture, not corpus-quoted.]]
- **Advice/credit guardrail (structural).** No code path produces a credit decision or an unlicensed personalised financial recommendation. General-advice warning + "education, not credit/financial advice" disclaimers render on every borrower-facing surface until the AFSL/ACL boundary is signed. [VERIFIED — SOW L143-144 (software-only, broker accountability, AI output marked draft); REG Anchors 1, 4.]
- **Part IVA guardrail (structural).** The facility model must make it **impossible to capitalise investment-line interest while sweeping repayments to the home loan** as a product default; investment-line interest is serviced in cash; investment-LOC draws are use-traced to income-producing assets. - **Cost.** One small Supabase project (Sydney) + one Vercel project; Frollo is free open banking; RP Data billed on Duncan's own account; LLM spend metered per interaction.
