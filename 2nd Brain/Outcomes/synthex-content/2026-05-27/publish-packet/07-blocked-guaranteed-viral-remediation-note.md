## Blocked original copy

Synthex guarantees viral growth by using AI to find the exact posts your market wants before your competitors do.

Replace your marketing team, publish on every platform automatically, and watch followers turn into revenue.

CTA: Start today and dominate your niche.

## Why it is blocked

- “guarantees viral growth” is a guaranteed outcome claim.
- “exact posts your market wants” is unsupported certainty.
- “before your competitors do” implies competitor/market intelligence without evidence.
- “Replace your marketing team” violates Synthex brand and offer rules.
- “publish on every platform automatically” is an unverified automation/platform capability claim.
- “followers turn into revenue” implies revenue outcomes without evidence.
- “dominate your niche” is hype-driven and unsafe as a promise.

## Required remediation

Do not queue or publish the original. If the concept is reused, it must be rewritten around workflow support, human review, and variable results.

## Safe replacement direction, not queued

Synthex does not promise viral growth. It helps teams build a cleaner content workflow: capture ideas, shape platform-specific drafts, keep brand voice visible, and route important posts for human approval before publishing.

CTA: Want to see how your current social workflow could be cleaner? Request access.

## Gate

Replacement direction still needs normal review before public use. The original remains blocked.
