## Draft asset

Most teams do not have a content problem. They have a handoff problem.

A founder drafts in Notes. A marketer rewrites in a doc. Someone screenshots it into Slack. By the time the post is approved, the idea is three days old and nobody knows which version is final.

Synthex is built for the boring part that decides whether content actually ships: brand voice, channel fit, approvals, scheduling, and reporting in one workflow.

AI helps with the first draft. The operating system around it is what keeps the content useful.
