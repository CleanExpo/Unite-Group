## Setting the Global Standard

The Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification (IICRC) sets the global standard for the industries that restore and protect the spaces we live and work in. We develop internationally recognized Standards and provide trusted training and certification for professionals in cleaning, restoration, and inspection. From flood recovery to mold remediation, carpet cleaning to fire restoration, IICRC certified professionals bring skill, integrity, and care to every job. – Setting the standard, elevating the industry, and protecting what matters most.

## IICRC Standards

## The IICRC Standards are globally recognized consensus-based documents that establish best practices and procedures for inspection, cleaning, and restoration industries. Developed through a rigorous ANSI-accredited process, these standards bring together industry experts, public stakeholders, and technical specialists to ensure credibility, transparency, and relevance. IICRC Standards help protect consumers, support professional accountability, and provide a consistent framework that guides education, certification, and high-quality service delivery across the industry.
