### A national mission to accelerate innovation and scientific discovery

AI for Science

Scientific discovery is the engine of human progress. But society’s most pressing challenges are more vast and complex than ever before.

They’re combinatorial puzzles – problems with so many possible answers that they’re practically impossible for traditional science to solve.

We believe AI is the right tool to decode these mysteries, and solve the root node challenges holding back entire fields of scientific knowledge.

Our blueprint is AlphaFold. In 2020, AlphaFold solved the protein-folding problem. Today, it’s used by over 3 million researchers, tackling problems like malaria and climate resistance.

**This is just the beginning.**

We’re now applying that same rigor to explore challenges from curing disease to achieving infinite clean energy.

With AI, scientists have a superpower to generate and test hypotheses at previously unimaginable scale and speed. With AI, nature becomes an informational system, where discoveries are made through a systematic, accelerated approach.

We don’t do it alone: we’re proud to work in deep collaboration with scientists around the globe. This is all part of our ambition to make sure AI-powered science benefits everyone.
