# GDBS

GDBS is a computational framework developed by [[VaultSync]] designed for high-precision modeling in [[Physics]], [[Pharmacology]], and [[Cosmology]]. The system operates via an **adaptive geometric representation**, utilizing a pure geometric substrate for complex calculations.

Unlike traditional computational methods, GDBS eliminates the requirement for [[Monte Carlo]] simulations and [[Feynman diagram]] integration. A primary demonstration of its efficacy is the computation of the five-loop anomalous magnetic moment of the electron ($a_e$) to a precision that exceeds current experimental standards.
