## Five operating constraints — non-negotiable

1. **NO AD SPEND. EVER.** [[synthex|Synthex]] produces all marketing in-house. Free + owned distribution only. CMO ad-spend gate retired 2026-05-13.
2. **VETTED CLIENTS ONLY.** Phill personally vets every onboarding. Quality over quantity — we say no more than yes.
3. **VIDEO-FIRST surfaces for Phill.** Founder has learning difficulties — every major status surface needs a video variant (NotebookLM daily briefing video at 7am AEST + Margot ElevenLabs voice replies).
4. **AGENTS EXECUTE.** Phill = think tank / ideas man. Margot → Pi-CEO Board → 25 senior agents own execution.
5. **THE $2B FILTER.** Every external artefact passes [[brand-guardian]]. Every strategic decision passes [[ceo-board]]. Every quote, claim, and citation is verifiable.
