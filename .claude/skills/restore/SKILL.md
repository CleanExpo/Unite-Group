# restore — RestoreAssist brand context

The software product for professional restoration contractors. Content inherits the
Unite-Group Nexus human voice (`~/2nd Brain/2nd Brain/Wiki/nexus-human-voice-2026-05-11.md`;
in-repo linter `apps/web/src/lib/brand/voice-rules.ts`) and layers the facts below.

## Brand

- **Name / URL:** RestoreAssist — `restoreassist.app` (NOT `.com.au`).
- **Tagline:** "One Software does all your Professional Restoration Needs."
- **Market:** Australian **and New Zealand** professional restoration industry. Australian built & designed.
- **Positioning:** all-in-one system — pull every part of a restoration job into ONE CRM.

## Voice

Nexus human voice, tuned for a **restoration business owner/technician** audience: practical,
field-credible, standards-literate. Open on a named operator (Karen and her five-van crew), show
the artefact (the scope, the moisture reading), earn the verdict. Not hype — the credibility engine
is the standards and the workflow, not adjectives.

## Key entities (real — trace every claim to these)

- **Three core pillars:** Business CRM · Job Management System · Scope and Quotes.
- **Six AI-assisted capabilities:** Photographs, Mapping, Documentation, Initial Inspection Reports, Scoping, Estimating/Quoting.
- **Standards workflows:** IICRC **S500** (water) and **S520** (mould) guided workflows.
- **On-site → office:** on-site capture (photos, moisture/meter readings, scope), AI report drafting, instant field↔office sync, client portal.
- **Integrations:** Xero, QuickBooks, ServiceM8, Ascora.
- **Data sovereignty:** "Your data, stored safe on your server" (self-hosted, on the customer's own server) — the key differentiator.

## What NOT to claim

- Do **not** write the domain as `.com.au` — it is `restoreassist.app`.
- Do **not** invent capabilities. If it is not in the six AI capabilities, the pillars, or the integration list, it does not exist yet.
- Do **not** say Australia-only — New Zealand is in scope.
- Do **not** claim IICRC *certifies or endorses the product*. The workflows are **guided by** S500/S520; the product is not an IICRC-accredited entity.
- Do **not** promise integrations beyond Xero / QuickBooks / ServiceM8 / Ascora without a source.
