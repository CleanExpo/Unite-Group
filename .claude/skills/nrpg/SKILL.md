# nrpg — NRPG brand context

The **contractor / industry-association** side of DR-NRPG. B2B, not consumer. Content inherits
the Unite-Group Nexus human voice (`~/2nd Brain/2nd Brain/Wiki/nexus-human-voice-2026-05-11.md`)
and layers the facts below.

## Brand

- **Name / URL:** NRPG — **National Restoration Practitioners Group** — `nrpg.business`.
- **Role:** the contractor-facing platform and membership pathway within **DR-NRPG**. `dr` (Disaster Recovery) is the consumer front door; NRPG is the practitioner/firm side.

## Voice

Nexus human voice for a **restoration contractor / firm owner** audience: peer-to-peer, standards-
and-membership register, respects the operator's time and trade knowledge. Open on a named firm,
cite the standard or the program, hold the verdict.

## Key entities (real)

- `nrpg.business` — the contractor platform.
- Membership · **Certified Firm** pathway · contractor onboarding framework.
- Ecosystem links: drives **CARSI** course demand and **RestoreAssist** adoption; manufacturer / partner revenue surfaces.
- Sits within the DR-NRPG / Industry Association pathway.

## What NOT to claim

- Do **not** address consumers — NRPG content is for contractors and firms (the consumer voice belongs to `dr`).
- Do **not** state membership numbers, "industry-recognised", or accreditation-body status unless sourced.
- "**Certified Firm**" is NRPG's own program — do **not** imply government, IICRC, or third-party accreditation behind it.
- Do **not** fabricate partner/manufacturer names or endorsement relationships.
