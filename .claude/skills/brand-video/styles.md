# brand-video — style registry ("the looks")

Single source of truth for the selectable video looks. Each `key` is a dropdown
value; `/brand-video style=<key>`. Add a look by appending a block — it becomes
selectable immediately. Machine-readable mirror: `styles.json` (keep in sync).

Every style shares the structural recipe (script → VO → 1 image/beat → stitch);
only the image POSITIVE/NEGATIVE tokens change. Audience notes guide selection.

---

## flat-line  ✅ validated (RestoreAssist, 9 videos)
**Audience:** B2B SaaS, trades/professional services, credible explainer.
POSITIVE: Clean modern flat-line explainer illustration, confident hand-drawn vector look, thick charcoal outlines, restrained palette of deep teal + warm amber + soft slate on off-white, single clear subject, generous white space, professional but human, subtle industry context.
NEGATIVE: not childish, not photorealistic, not a 3D render, not a photograph, no gradients, no text, no logos, no clutter.

## hand-doodle
**Audience:** broad/educational, social, "explainer YouTube" (Zen/whiteboard feel).
POSITIVE: Hand-drawn marker doodle illustration, loose sketchy black ink lines on white, simple friendly characters, light spot-colour highlights, whiteboard-explainer energy, single clear subject.
NEGATIVE: not photorealistic, not a 3D render, not corporate-stock, no gradients, no text, no logos, no clutter.

## bold-kinetic
**Audience:** paid social/ads, high-scroll-stop, younger/consumer.
POSITIVE: Bold flat colour-block illustration, high-contrast saturated palette, thick geometric shapes, strong diagonal composition, punchy poster energy, single dominant subject, lots of negative space for motion.
NEGATIVE: not muted, not photorealistic, not a 3D render, no gradients, no text, no logos, no fine detail clutter.

## cinematic-photoreal
**Audience:** premium brand, hero/launch films, trust & aspiration.
POSITIVE: Cinematic photorealistic still, shallow depth of field, dramatic directional lighting, filmic colour grade, real-world environment, single clear focal subject, premium editorial mood.
NEGATIVE: not cartoon, not illustration, not flat, no text overlays, no logos, no watermark, no cluttered backgrounds.

## minimal-corporate
**Audience:** enterprise/finance/compliance, calm authority.
POSITIVE: Minimal corporate illustration, clean line + single accent colour on white, abundant whitespace, precise geometric icons, restrained and trustworthy, one clear subject.
NEGATIVE: not busy, not childish, not photorealistic, no gradients, no text, no logos, no clutter.

## retro-print
**Audience:** distinctive/memorable brand, indie/craft, scroll-stopping difference.
POSITIVE: Warm retro mid-century print-poster illustration, limited risograph-style palette, visible halftone texture, bold simplified shapes, nostalgic editorial feel, single clear subject.
NEGATIVE: not photorealistic, not 3D, not glossy, no text, no logos, no modern flat-gradient look, no clutter.
