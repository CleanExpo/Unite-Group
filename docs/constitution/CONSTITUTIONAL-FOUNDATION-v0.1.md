## Preamble

We believe every person has greater potential than they currently recognise.

Human potential is not fixed by circumstance or current knowledge. It expands when people gain access
to trusted knowledge, practical understanding, and the confidence to act.

**We exist to remove barriers between curiosity and capability.**

We turn information into understanding, understanding into confident action, and action into lasting
improvement.

**Technology, business, and artificial intelligence are instruments, not the mission.**

Our mission is **human potential, realised through integrity, evidence, and continuous learning**.

**Our success is measured not by dependency created, but by capability expanded.**

---

## First Principles

Principles **follow from** the mission. They are not invented.

1. **Human potential first.**
2. **Truth before convenience.**
3. **Empowerment over dependency.**
4. **Learning never ends.**
5. **Integrity through transparency.**
6. **Long-term stewardship.**
7. **Challenge assumptions.**

Principle 7 creates the built-in balance: **stable mission, evolving understanding.**

## The falsifiable commitment

**"Success is measured not by dependency created, but by capability expanded"** is the only line in
this document that can be **failed**. Everything else is a value; this is a test. It is kept because it
can be failed.

**Current state, recorded honestly on the day of locking: it is failing.**

**Evidence.** As of 2026-07-17 the estate's doctrine **does not inherit**. `~/.claude/hooks/` and
`settings.json` are gitignored and do not propagate; `bootstrap.sh` wires **1 of 3** enforcement
installers and swallows failure into an echo; **47 of 291 skills are uncatalogued — including
`proof-discipline` and `goal-circuit-breaker`, the guards that enforce this doctrine**; the knowledge
graph holds **681 rows and has no reader**. A fresh machine, a new agent, or a new colleague inherits
**nothing — silently**.

Meanwhile the estate's constitution, nine decision records, a 66-defect reconciliation, and four
capability specs were produced in a single session in which **momentum was 100% human-supplied**
(ARR-008 §8.1). **Capability was expressed. It was not yet expanded** — because almost none of it
reaches the next person or machine.

**That is dependency created, measured by this document's own standard, on this estate's own evidence.**

**Recorded on day one rather than discovered later.** A test that only gets cited when it passes is not
a test. See `docs/decisions/ARR-007-organisational-knowledge-and-doctrine.md` §5.1 and §9.
