## Final approved draft — v3 wording only

The hard part of social is not writing one good post. It is keeping every channel moving without turning the team into a copy-paste machine.

Synthex helps bring the work into one place: campaign planning, brand voice, channel-aware drafts, review notes, and approval handoffs.

That matters most when one idea needs several useful versions: a professional post, a short update, a founder note, a blog outline, or a client summary.

The goal is not more content for its own sake. It is fewer dropped ideas, fewer messy approvals, and a clearer path from draft to review.
