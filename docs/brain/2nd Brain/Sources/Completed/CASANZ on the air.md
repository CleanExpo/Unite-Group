## CASANZ is on the Air - Podcast Series

Dive into the vibrant discussions, expert insights, and engaging stories from the CASANZ Podcast Series, recorded live in Hobart during the 27th International Clean Air Conference 2024. This exclusive series brings together thought leaders, innovators, and industry pioneers to explore critical air quality challenges and solutions shaping our world. From the personal journeys of starting air quality consultancies to exploring groundbreaking research and policy, each episode offers a unique perspective on fostering cleaner, healthier air. Whether you're a seasoned professional, an emerging expert, or simply passionate about making a difference, these conversations will leave you inspired and informed. We will be releasing one episode daily from December 5th to 23rd—your perfect dose of inspiration and insight to wrap up the year! This fantastic initiative was made possible thanks to the support and sponsorship of Zephyr Environmental.
