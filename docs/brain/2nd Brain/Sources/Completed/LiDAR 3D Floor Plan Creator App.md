## LiDAR 3D Floor Plan Creator

Tape Measure & Room Scanner

Free · In-App Purchases · Designed for iPad. Not verified for macOS.

LiDAR Plan – Fast & Accurate Room Scanning LiDAR Plan is a simple yet powerful app for measuring spaces and creating room plans using LiDAR technology. Whether you're planning a renovation, furnishing a room, or capturing dimensions on the go, LiDAR Plan helps you do it quickly and precisely. Key Features: • Room Plan 3D – Generate 3D room layouts with accurate dimensions • Ruler & Area – Measure distances and surface areas using AR • Export – Save your scans in convenient formats like PDF or USDZ • Measurement History – Access and manage your saved scans anytime • Clean Interface – Designed for ease of use and speed Perfect for home projects, quick surveys, interior planning, and more. LiDAR Plan – Your essential tool for fast, precise, and portable space measurement. 3D scanning mode is only available on devices equipped with a LiDAR sensor that supports 3D scanning, such as the iPhone 12 Pro, iPad Pro, and later Pro or Pro Max models.
