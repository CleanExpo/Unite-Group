##### Council of Small Business Organisations Australia

## COSBOA is the Voice for Small Business

The Council of Small Business Organisations Australia (COSBOA) is Australia’s peak body exclusively representing small businesses. With over 50 member associations nationally, we provide unparalleled advocacy and support to empower small businesses who lack the time and resources to navigate constant legislative and regulatory changes.

## COSBOA 2026 Small Business Perspectives Survey is now open, and we want to hear from you

This year’s survey has a dedicated focus on regional, rural, and remote businesses to make sure your experiences are reflected in national policy discussions.

The findings will directly shape COSBOA’s advocacy on the issues that affect you most, including tax, workforce, housing, energy, connectivity, insurance, and regulatory reform.

##### COSBOA Membership

## Become a Member Today

COSBOA has two tiers of membership available. Members have the opportunity to play an active role in shaping the future of Australia’s small business community.  

COSBOA delivers a range of activities, programs and events that support our members and small businesses in Australia. Members are ultimately at the heart of what we do, with our associations informing our agenda and priorities.
