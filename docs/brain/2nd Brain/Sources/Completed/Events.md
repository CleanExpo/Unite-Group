### We present industry leading events, including exhibitions, conferences and award ceremonies for business audiences. We have over 30 annual events attended by 100,000 professionals.

Our experienced event organisers manage every step of the event or conference – from sourcing topics and recruiting speakers and guests to securing advertising and sponsorship.

Our events are critical for networking, product showcases, and celebrating each industry and the people behind them. We connect with professionals in Australia and New Zealand, as well as globally through the AHICE network.
