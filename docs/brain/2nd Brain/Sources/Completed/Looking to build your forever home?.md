Not your neighbour’s builder.

We’re here to cater to people who have the vision of thinking differently about housing. Working with you, we can design and build your dream house, tailored to your individual requirements. Alternatively, we can work alongside you in your self-build, supporting you to navigate the once-in-a-lifetime opportunity of creating what you want, the way you want it. Let us take the stress and technicalities out of an experience that should be exciting and inspiring, so you can focus on the things that matter, knowing that the final result will be outstanding - created with your input and finished to the highest standard possible.

Bespoke, sustainable, functional, beautiful - BLD SCI caters to a small clientele of discerning individuals. You and your build deserve professional, personalised care and attention. Working with BLD SCI, you can be assured that your needs will be heard and honoured, and your design and delivery requirements met. We pride ourselves on developing strong, positive relationships with our clients, so we can exceed expectations and create a truly incredible outcome - each and every time.

Sustainability and treading lightly on our magical planet is also crucial to us. We take our responsibility to care for the earth and environment very seriously, meaning we are committed to constantly monitoring, reviewing, and improving our practices. So you can be assured that your build will have minimal impact on both the local ecosystem and global environment.

Our long-term goal is to make sustainable housing accessible, affordable, and standard.

We’re also experienced in meeting complex sustainability and accessibility requirements, so your build goes ahead smoothly and efficiently, without the cost and hassle of wasted time.

The common thread tying the stories of each of our builds together is one of durability, beauty, functionality, and human-centred design, that seamlessly blends into the surrounding natural environment with minimal impact - so you can be truly at home, in your home.

Looking to build your forever home?

Not just a house, but a place to call home.
