If you didn’t document it, did it even happen?!  
Property restorers have enough to worry about on a job site without having to worry about a clunky documentation process. The Encircle app is specifically designed to help contractors capture visual documentation in the field. Get a walk-through of the Encircle features that help restoration field teams capture what they need, quickly.  
  
This 30-minute session includes:  
Capturing all of the photos you need, including overview photos and close-up/detailed views.  
Leveraging photo notes to capture justification billable line items like PPE and equipment.  
Using video to provide a guided walkthrough with expert narrative.  
Create an initial inspection report before you’ve even left the property.  
Tips and tricks for getting the most out of Encircle.  
  
Find out more at https://getencircle.com/
