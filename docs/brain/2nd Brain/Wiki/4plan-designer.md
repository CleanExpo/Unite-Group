# 4plan Designer

**Status: candidate — not yet a portfolio business.** Listed in [[businesses-overview]] as conceptual/design-phase. Filed here so the wikilink resolves and the concept stays tracked.

## Concept

Home interior design / renovation app targeting consumers and renovators rather than restoration contractors. Distinct from the [[floor-plan-workstream]] (which serves [[restore-assist]] field techs) — 4plan would be the renovation-side sibling.

## Why It Sits In The Same Category

The acquisition technology — Apple RoomPlan, LiDAR, smartphone-video stitching — is identical across restoration and renovation. The differentiator is the overlay: restoration uses IICRC damage categories ([[iicrc-content-initiative]]); renovation uses material catalogs, finishes, photorealistic rendering. Same engine, different overlay.

## Market Examples

The Mindful Builder Network provides visual context for the target user base and renovation scope.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=embed)

The target market includes discerning individuals seeking bespoke, sustainable, and highly functional homes. Services must cater to personalized care, supporting self-builds or full design/build cycles. Key requirements include:
*   **Sustainability:** Commitment to minimal environmental impact and responsible building practices.
*   **Functionality:** Designing spaces that are durable, beautiful, and human-centered, blending seamlessly with the natural environment.
*   **Complexity:** Ability to manage and meet complex sustainability and accessibility requirements efficiently.
