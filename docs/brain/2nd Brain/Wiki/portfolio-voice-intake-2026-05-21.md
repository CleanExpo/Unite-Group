# Portfolio Voice Intake

Purpose: convert the new Plaud recordings into a durable instruction set for Margot, Unite-Group CRM, Synthex, RestoreAssist, and DR/NRPG.

## Voice Intake Mandate

Plaud, Telegram, and future phone/voice-agent inputs are not note dumps. They are raw founder intent. The system must transform them into:

- a structured brief
- linked business context
- owner and risk state
- execution route
- Wiki evidence
- CRM/Linear/Kanban task
- verification gate

The first durable write remains [[unite-crm]] or the operating Wiki. Synthex receives the work only when the classifier says the task is marketing, campaign, content, SEO/AEO/GEO, or client-presentation related.

## Meeting Cockpit Pattern

From the 2026-05-17 Plaud meeting:

- A live meeting sandbox should show current conversation state, research activity, competitor lookup, idea breakdown, draft visuals, and proposal assets while the meeting is happening.
- Margot acts as the go-getter: internet research, Apify/Semrush/DataForSEO/Google Analytics pulls, company context, competitor scan, SWOT, landing-page mock, storyboard, campaign draft, and proposal packet.
- The close motion is proof, not persuasion: before the meeting ends the prospect should see enough structured output to trust the system.
- Pricing anchor recorded: known/referral client baseline around AUD 2,750/month including GST; dry client minimum around AUD 3,300/month; target profit 70-80% depending on acquisition warmth and support load.

## RestoreAssist Dictation Pattern

RestoreAssist should support field and admin voice capture as an add-on:

- technician site conversations
- customer statements
- assessor/builder/trade conversations
- equipment, chemicals, safety, measurements, photos, work completed, work remaining
- admin phone intake and follow-up calls

The transcript becomes inspection evidence, CRM history, and structured reporting support. Any cross-client data use must be aggregated, consent-safe, and stripped of private details before being used for industry education, Google E-E-A-T content, trend reports, or marketing campaigns.

## NRPG Member Value Pattern

NRPG membership must answer the contractor question: "What is in it for me?"

Value stack:

- actionable service information members can apply immediately
- industry podcast/audio recommendations for owners on the road
- member-only business-growth, marketing, wealth, and operations learning tracks
- contractor tools that raise business value and local presence
- feedback loops that make members feel heard and invested
- measurable 10x perceived value against membership spend

## Personalized Content Dashboard

Unite-Group should develop a founder/member learning surface that recommends podcasts, audiobooks, books, and industry content based on listening/reading history and declared objectives.

Initial content lanes:

- psychology of marketing and branding
- business growth
- wealth building and money management
- empire building
- association operation
- solopreneur and entrepreneur systems
- indoor environmental, restoration, construction, plumbing, and AI industry podcasts

Access pattern: mobile-first, road-safe, one-tap playback or queueing through existing podcast/audiobook apps where permissions allow. Any Amazon/Spotify/podcast integration must be credential-gated and privacy-reviewed.
