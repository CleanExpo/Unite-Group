## The unifying play

The $2B is not five SaaS products. It is one industry association — the [[industry-association-vision-2026]] — with five products and a paying client as its operational substrate. NRPG expanded from restoration into all cleaning / restoration / property services trades. Toby ([[ccw]]) co-founds. The full vertical stack: equipment → training → certification → jobs → insurance → technology.
