# The ITR Button: A Referral-Based Financial Ecosystem

![PLAUD NOTE](permanent/856abf5610d24ffda635fae8ce8f1598/mem_do3dJJo8vL/summary_poster/card_20260620042233-v2@c0d0afcc544009f4dd9068_20260620042356_9d988492.png)

This transcript outlines a business model for an AI-driven "ITR Button," a sophisticated referral system designed to onboard individual taxpayers with a low-cost, AI-guided tax return service. The system then systematically refers them to a network of professionals—including accountants, mortgage brokers, and financial planners—for higher-value services. It utilizes two AI chatbots, Dmitri (pre-lodgement) and Noah (post-lodgement), to manage the client journey and facilitate ongoing communication through a standardized "Summary, Recommendation, and Timeline" (SRT) document. This process creates a self-sustaining, client-centric ecosystem of professionals.

------------
## The Initial Client Engagement: The "Dmitri" Onboarding Process
This section details the initial stage of the client journey, which begins when a user, such as the example persona "Mary," conducts an online search for low-cost or fast individual tax return services. The AI chatbot "Dmitri" is designed to intercept these search queries and present itself as a "purpose-based referral led" service. Its primary function is to guide the user through completing their tax return via the official myGov ATO portal for a nominal fee of thirty dollars. Dmitri assists with downloading pre-fill data and navigating the tax return, answering basic questions by drawing from its own closed-server knowledge base. Crucially, this process is structured to avoid handling sensitive information like Tax File Numbers (TFNs) or personal ID, thereby bypassing stringent AML (Anti-Money Laundering) and ID Privacy Act requirements. This automated onboarding saves significant time and money for future professional partners, allowing them to offer more value-driven services at a competitive price.

------------
## The Post-Lodgement and Referral Engine: The "Noah" and "SRT" System
This section explains the transition from the pre-lodgement chatbot, Dmitri, to the post-lodgement AI, "Noah," an acronym for "Notice of Assessment." After Mary pays the thirty-dollar fee and lodges her return, Noah takes over as a "sales chatbot" to analyze her financial data. Its objective is to identify opportunities for financial improvement and wealth creation, such as unused "carry forward concessional contributions" or inefficient savings. The core of this process is the "SRT" (Summary, Recommendation, and Timeline), a standardized report that outlines the client's situation, recommends professional engagement, and sets a timeline for action. This document, which contains no TFNs or personal ID, is then used to refer Mary to vetted professionals like April, an accountant at Blink's, and Jack, a mortgage broker at Home Loan Essentials. The system is designed with a "what's next" protocol, ensuring client files remain perpetually open to track progress and guarantee follow-through from all parties.

------------
## The Professional Network in Action: The Accountant's Role
This section illustrates how the referred accountant, April, engages with the client, Mary, following the prompt from Noah's SRT. The process creates a recurring cycle of value-added engagement. April conducts a tax planning session in February, well before the end of the financial year, to discuss strategies like salary sacrifice packages and income protection. This proactive planning, costing Mary a tax-deductible fee of $220, results in a significantly better tax outcome—a $2,000 refund compared to her previous $200 refund. Because Dmitri and Noah handle the initial data collection, April can offer her services at a reduced rate (e.g., $180 instead of $220). The system's perpetual feedback loop requires April to submit an SRT back to Noah, detailing the next steps and timeline, ensuring continuous and timely client management. Even if April is unavailable, the referral is directed to her firm, maintaining service continuity.

------------
## Expanding the Ecosystem: The Mortgage Broker and Beyond
This section describes the parallel referral process to other professionals, using mortgage broker "Jack" from Home Loan Essentials as an example. Noah sends a simultaneous SRT to Jack, flagging that Mary is paying tax on interest earned from her savings. Jack engages with Mary to refinance her home loan, establish an offset account to eliminate the tax on her savings, and introduce her to advanced products like a "DIY home loan" for debt recycling. This intervention not only saves Mary money but also improves her overall financial position. Noah acts as an automated calendar, tracking all open SRTs and nudging professionals like Jack to ensure timely follow-ups. As Mary's life evolves (e.g., she becomes pregnant), Jack refers her to a financial planner to address insurance and estate planning needs, summarized by the acronym "Fitter" (Finance, Insurance, Taxation, Trusts, Estate, and Retirement planning). This expansion demonstrates the system's viral growth, as impressed professionals like the planner and their associated lawyers seek to integrate the ITR button into their own practices, creating a "satellite" of experts orbiting the client.

------------
## Long-Term Vision and Scalability: The Self-Sustaining Satellite Model
This final section outlines the long-term vision for the ITR button, which is to build a scalable, self-sustaining "satellite of professionals" around each client. With the client positioned as the central "Earth," this network of accountants, brokers, planners, and lawyers is coordinated by Noah, which uses the SRT system to prompt consistent and timely engagement. This model is designed for infinite replication; as client volume grows, the system can manage capacity by auctioning leads to build new, geographically specific satellites in locations like Townsville or even for expatriate communities in Dubai. Professionals can pay to have the ITR button on their websites, feeding into and benefiting from this expanding ecosystem. The entire structure is built on a foundation of continuous communication and follow-up, ensuring clients are perpetually managed and supported through every stage of their financial lives.
