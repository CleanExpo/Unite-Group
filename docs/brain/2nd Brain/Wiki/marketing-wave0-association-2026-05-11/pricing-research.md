# Pricing Research — Expanded NRPG Industry Association (Wave 0)

> **INTERNAL — Unite-Group portfolio only — do not distribute publicly 2026-05-11**

Verifies the **provisional** $499 / $1,499 / $3,999+ tiers proposed in `nrpg-association-wave0-2026-05-11.json` against real competitor dues, ANZ B2B SaaS bundle prices, and the Toby/CCW willingness-to-pay anchor.

**Headline finding:** the provisional Founder tier was **roughly 40-60% too high**; the Firm tier was **roughly correct but mis-anchored** (overpriced vs association dues, underpriced vs the agency-replacement value prop it actually delivers); the Enterprise tier was **directionally right but the floor should drop**. Recommended verified tier-sheet appears at the bottom. Single biggest correction: **drop the Founder tier from $499 → $249/mo with a 100-firm cap and lifetime rate-lock** — the cap × rate-lock is the conversion lever, not the headline price.

FX rate applied throughout: **1 USD = 1.380 AUD** (Reserve Bank of Australia / [exchange-rates.org May 2026 history](https://www.exchange-rates.org/exchange-rate-history/usd-aud-2026), AUD/USD = 0.7245 on 8 May 2026). USD source numbers are converted, then rounded to the nearest $5.

## 5. Recommended tier-sheet (post-research)

The provisional tiers in the wave-plan json were:

| Provisional (UNVERIFIED) | Price/mo (AUD) | Cap | Includes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder | $499 | 50 | All services + advisory seat + branding rights |
| Firm | $1,499 | none | Cert + leads + marketing + tools + insurance + equipment bundle |
| Enterprise (from) | $3,999 | none | Firm + custom insurer-panel routing + white-label |

### Verified-data corrections

The provisional pricing has **three calibration errors**:

1. **Founder tier $499/mo (AU$5,988/yr) is too high vs the association-dues comparator.** No restoration / cleaning body in the data set charges $5,988/yr for any membership at any tier. The closest is NADCA Associate at AU$2,132/yr ($178/mo). Founder tier needs to *underprice* the comparison association-dues set while *over-delivering* on the bundle. **Right number: AU$249/mo founder-locked.** This sits at ~1.3× the NADCA Associate price for a vastly bigger bundle and is below the "is this real?" credibility floor (sub-$200/mo reads as "weekend project association"). The cap × lifetime rate-lock is the conversion lever — not the headline number.
2. **Firm tier $1,499/mo is plausible BUT mis-anchored.** It's far above any association-dues comparator (top: ~AU$2,000/yr / $167/mo for HIA standard tier) but **below the agency-retainer floor it actually competes with** ($1,500-$5,000/mo for AU SMB marketing retainer). The price is defensible against the *agency replacement* value prop, NOT the *association membership* value prop. **Right number: AU$1,200/mo standard Firm.** Anchors at the *bottom* of the agency-retainer band, well above any association, and lets the sales conversation lean on the marketing-DFY value (which `icp-research.md` confirms is Pain #3, universal and pre-loaded).
3. **Enterprise tier $3,999/mo floor is correct in shape but should drop to AU$2,500/mo entry.** This is *because* the agency-retainer comparator ceiling is AU$5,000-$6,000/mo and we need the Enterprise floor to undercut a typical full-service agency engagement while the ceiling can stretch to $8,000+/mo on quote. $3,999 starts the conversation above where the buyer expects; $2,500 starts it below and lets the modules upsell.

### 5b. Why add a Sole-Trader / Associate tier

ICP-research correctly identifies single-operator sole-trader carpet cleaners (1-2 person, <AU$300k revenue) as **Anti-ICP** for the Firm tier. But: (a) the cleaning side of the market is genuinely sole-trader-heavy, (b) carpet-only operators are the natural "graduating into restoration" pipeline, and (c) at AU$79/mo the tier funds the directory + LMS + advocacy slice without diluting the Firm tier value prop. Tier-gated *without* lead routing and *without* the marketing bundle to keep it from cannibalising Firm conversions. Verifies against the IICRC Certified Firm + RIA per-branch ($299/yr = $25/mo) entry-level association price band, just slightly above.

### 5c. Per-tier value justification (anchored on the research)

- **Founder AU$249/mo** vs comparator: 1.4× NADCA Associate (AU$178/mo) for an order-of-magnitude bigger bundle; sits *below* a single Xactimate seat (~AU$240-$280/mo) — the price is "less than what you already pay Verisk monthly". Lifetime rate-lock is the moat.
- **Firm AU$1,200/mo** vs comparator: ~80% of the cheapest tradie-focused full-service agency retainer (AU$1,500/mo, [20minutemarketing](https://www.20minutemarketing.com.au/blog/digital-marketing-for-tradies-australia-2026)); ~24% of the agency-retainer ceiling (AU$5,000/mo); ~10× the most expensive single-body dues ($167/mo HIA) for a bundle that delivers cert + leads + marketing + AI tools + advocacy + group-buy — none of which the dues-only competitors deliver.
- **Enterprise from AU$2,500/mo** vs comparator: undercuts the typical full-service SMB agency retainer ceiling (AU$5,000-$6,000/mo) while delivering insurer-panel routing + white-label cert delivery no agency can offer. The "from" anchor lets sales scale to AU$8,000-$15,000/mo on franchise / multi-state quotes.
- **Sole-trader AU$79/mo** vs comparator: 3× IICRC Certified Firm price (AU$199/yr ≈ $17/mo) for a multi-cert bundle + LMS + directory — strictly priced to keep the entry-level credible without competing with Firm.

## 6. $12M ARR moat — sanity-check

Original moat math (per [[competitor-service-stack-2026-05-11]]): 1,000 members × ~AU$1,000/mo avg = AU$12M ARR.

### Revised math with verified tiers

Plausible Year-3 steady-state member mix (post-launch, post-Coutis, post-association maturity):

| Tier | Count | AUD/mo | Annual contribution |
|---|---|---|---|
| Founder | 100 (cap) | $249 | AU$298,800 |
| Firm | 750 | $1,200 | AU$10,800,000 |
| Enterprise | 50 | $3,500 (blended above $2,500 floor) | AU$2,100,000 |
| Sole-trader | 500 | $79 | AU$474,000 |
| **Total members** | **1,400** | — | **AU$13,672,800 ARR** |

**Verdict: ACHIEVABLE — slightly understated.** The original $12M target gets crossed at **~830 Firm-tier members + Founder cap + 30 Enterprise + 300 Sole-traders**, i.e. ~1,260 total members across tiers. The expanded ICP from `icp-research.md` (5-50 FTE ANZ restoration/cleaning + adjacent property-services trades) sustains this comfortably IF the agency-replacement / member-services value prop converts at the Firm tier as ICP-pain-#3 predicts.

**Downside scenario** (Firm-tier conversion underperforms; bundle reads as another association rather than agency-replacement): 200 Firm × $1,200 + Founder cap × $249 + 30 Enterprise × $3,500 + 800 Sole × $79 = **AU$3,990k ARR** — i.e. ~33% of target. The Firm-tier conversion is the single variable that decides the whole thesis. The wave-0 launch copy / positioning carries the load.

**Upside scenario** (white-label Enterprise for franchise networks lands 3-5 deals): 100 Founder + 1,000 Firm + 100 Enterprise × $4,500 blended (with franchise/state-level whitelabel pricing) + 600 Sole = **AU$19.3M ARR**.

### Single biggest sanity-check finding

**The math hits $12M only if the Firm tier averages 700-800 paying members.** That's roughly **1.4-1.6%** of the addressable ANZ restoration/specialty-cleaning + adjacent property-services trade firm count (industry-cited ~50,000 firms across cert-aligned categories). That's plausible but not automatic — requires (a) Toby/CCW + 5-10 founding partners' word-of-mouth in Wave 0-1, (b) the marketing-DFY pillar delivering measurable ROI to early Firm members, (c) the Coutis halo holding through 24-month onboarding window, (d) at least 2 insurer-panel wins to validate the lead-routing claim.
