### Trade-mark verdict

**Mixed — proceed on full wordmark, hold off on the bare acronym.**

- Full wordmark **"AUSTRALIAN TRADE INDUSTRY ASSOCIATION"** is clear and registrable in classes 35, 41, 42, 45.
- Bare acronym **"ATIA"** is in conflict with two live marks held by the Australian Federation of Travel Agents in class 41 and overlapping classes — same acronym, similar institutional context (industry association). An IP examiner would likely raise this.
- Recommend filing the **full wordmark first** in classes 35 (business / association services), 41 (education / training / certification), 42 (scientific / technical / standards) and 45 (legal / advocacy). Defer ATIA-acronym filing until a trade-mark attorney has assessed coexistence risk with the Federation of Travel Agents.
- A coexistence opinion from a trade-mark attorney is the next external dependency.
