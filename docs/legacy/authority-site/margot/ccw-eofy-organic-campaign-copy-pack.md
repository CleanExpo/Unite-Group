## Safety and compliance guardrails

- Do not state or imply guaranteed tax credits, deductions, write-offs, savings, or accountant outcomes.
- Use accountant-safe wording only: "speak with your accountant", "eligible businesses may be able to claim", "plan purchases before 30 June", "before EOFY".
- No paid ad spend is approved by this pack.
- No scheduling or posting is approved by this pack.
- Route final approved copy through Synthex/scheduling after Toby/Phill approval.
- Swap product names, stock counts, pricing, photos, and service availability before publication.

## Campaign positioning

Core message: EOFY is a practical planning moment for cleaning businesses to review equipment, consumables, machines, repairs, and servicing before 30 June.

Primary channels:

1. Facebook first.
2. LinkedIn variants for business/procurement tone.
3. Instagram variants with shorter captions and visual-first product/service hooks.

Audience:

- Commercial cleaners
- Carpet cleaners
- Restoration/maintenance operators
- Facilities and operations managers
- Small business owners planning purchases before EOFY

## Required inputs before scheduling

- Current CCW stock list and best-margin items.
- Machines/products Toby wants prioritised.
- Service workshop booking capacity for the next 2–4 weeks.
- Any EOFY trading-hour changes.
- Approved product images/video clips.
