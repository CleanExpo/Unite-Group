# UNI-2053 Decision: CCW First Product Category

> Date: 2026-05-30  
> Decision owner: Margot (agent-drafted) / Phill (sign-off)  
> Client: CCW (Toby)

## Context

CCW needs its first product category page and content pack for web, social, and SEO. The draft at `docs/margot/ccw-carpet-cleaning-machines-category-copy.md` exists but the category decision itself needs locking.

## Options

| # | Category | Rationale For | Rationale Against |
|---|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| A | **Carpet Cleaning Machines & Extractors** | Core product; highest search intent; highest margin; flagship category; draft already exists | May be too broad if Toby wants to start narrower |
| B | **Carpet Cleaning Chemicals & Solutions** | High repeat purchase; consumables = recurring revenue; lower price point = lower barrier to first sale | Lower margin; less "hero" content; harder to differentiate |
| C | **Spot & Stain Removal Products** | Niche; specific problem-solving angle; great for PPC/social ads; easy to rank for long-tail keywords | Smaller audience; not flagship; may pigeonhole CCW |

## Decision: **A. Carpet Cleaning Machines & Extractors**

**Why this wins**
1. **Search dominance** — "carpet cleaning machines" and "carpet extractors" are the highest-volume keywords in CCW's space. Starting here captures the most organic traffic.
2. **Margin leadership** — Machines are CCW's highest-ticket, highest-margin items. One machine sale = months of chemical revenue.
3. **Brand positioning** — CCW is known for machines. Leading with chemicals or spot removers would confuse the brand identity.
4. **Content leverage** — Machine content naturally cross-sells chemicals, wands, and accessories. The reverse is harder.
5. **Draft already complete** — 205 lines of copy across web, social (Facebook ×3, LinkedIn, Instagram), SEO meta, and shot list. Ready to publish once Toby confirms stock/CTA.

## What this unlocks

- Phase 1 content pack is approved → move to Synthex scheduling queue
- Sets the template for all future category pages (chemicals, spot removal, upholstery, floor care)
- SEO foundation is laid from day one
